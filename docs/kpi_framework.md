# KPI Framework
*Definitions, Formulas, and Alert Thresholds for the Olist Marketplace Intelligence Platform*

This document defines the metrics calculated and presented across the four stakeholder dashboards.

---

## 1. Executive / CEO KPIs

### Total Revenue
* **Definition**: Total dollar volume processed by the platform, representing Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) or total payment received.
* **SQL Logic**: `SUM(total_payment)` from `fact_orders`
* **Business Purpose**: Tracks top-line growth.

### Total Orders
* **Definition**: Total volume of transaction transactions completed on the marketplace.
* **SQL Logic**: `COUNT(order_id)` from `fact_orders`
* **Business Purpose**: Measures transaction activity.

### Average Order Value (AOV)
* **Definition**: Average value spent by a customer per transaction.
* **Formula**: `Total Revenue / Total Orders`
* **Target Benchmark**: **150 BRL** (Alert if AOV falls below 140 BRL)

### Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth
* **Definition**: The percentage change in revenue or orders compared to the previous month.
* **Formula**: `((Current Month - Previous Month) / Previous Month) * 100` (Calculated using the `LAG()` window function).
* **Target Benchmark**: **+5% MoM** (Alert if growth turns negative).

---

## 2. Operations / Logistics KPIs

### On-Time Delivery Rate (OTD%)
* **Definition**: The percentage of customer orders delivered on or before the estimated delivery date promised at checkout.
* **Formula**: `(Orders where actual_delivery_days <= estimated_delivery_days) / Total Delivered Orders * 100`
* **SLA Target**: **95%** (Alert turns RED if OTD% falls below 90%).

### Average Delivery Days
* **Definition**: The average time elapsed from customer purchase timestamp to carrier delivery timestamp.
* **Formula**: `AVG(delivered_customer_timestamp - purchase_timestamp)` (expressed in fractional days).
* **Target Benchmark**: **10.0 Days** (Alert turns RED if average exceeds 12.0 days).

### Late Delivery Rate (LDR%)
* **Definition**: The percentage of orders arriving after the estimated delivery date.
* **Formula**: `(Orders where actual_delivery_days > estimated_delivery_days) / Total Delivered Orders * 100`
* **Target Benchmark**: **< 5%** (Alert turns RED if LDR% exceeds 10%).

---

## 3. Marketing & Customer KPIs

### Repeat Order Share (RPR%)
* **Definition**: The percentage of total monthly orders placed by returning customers (customers who have already purchased at least once before).
* **Formula**: `(Orders where customer_order_number > 1) / Total Orders * 100` (Calculated using `ROW_NUMBER()`).
* **Target Benchmark**: **5%** (Olist has a low repeat share, hover alert displays warning below 3%).

### Average Review Score
* **Definition**: The average rating score left by customers on order reviews.
* **SQL Logic**: `AVG(review_score::numeric)` from `raw_order_reviews`
* **Target Benchmark**: **4.2 / 5.0** (Alert turns RED if score drops below 4.0).

---

## 4. Geographic & Category KPIs

### Revenue Concentration
* **Definition**: The percentage share of platform revenue generated by the top states.
* **Formula**: `(State Revenue / Total Revenue) * 100` (Calculated using the window function `SUM(revenue) OVER ()`).
* **Risk Threshold**: High risk if any single state exceeds **40%** of total revenue. (São Paulo currently holds ~37% concentration).
