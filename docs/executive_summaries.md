# Executive Summaries & Action Items
*Stakeholder-Specific Reports & Recommendations*

---

## 📊 Executive Summary for the CEO
**Core Question**: Is the marketplace healthy and growing?

### Key Findings:
1. **Growth Bottlenecks**: Overall monthly order volumes are growing (e.g., reaching **6,421 orders** in August 2018), but **revenue growth is not keeping pace with orders** (-4.13% drop in revenue in August 2018). This is dragging down our Average Order Value.
2. **Customer Churn Risk**: Olist is operating as a "leaky bucket." We acquire nearly **6,000 to 7,000 new customers monthly**, but **under 0.5% return** for a second purchase.
3. **Logistics Vulnerability**: When delivery bottlenecks occur (such as in March 2018), customer ratings drop immediately, which stunts word-of-mouth growth.

### Recommendations:
1. Approve budget allocation for a customer loyalty/points program. Retaining a customer yields a **2x higher Average Order Value** than acquiring a new customer.
2. Direct the VP of Operations to investigate carrier SLAs on long-distance routes.

## 🚚 Executive Summary for the VP of Operations
**Core Question**: Are we delivering on our promise?

### Key Findings:
1. **Logistics Performance**: Overall Average Delivery Days is healthy (~7.7 days in August 2018), but the system is vulnerable to sudden bottlenecks (e.g., in March 2018, delivery days doubled to **16.2 days**, and late delivery rates spiked to **21.15%**).
2. **Root Cause Analysis**: 
   * **Route SP ➔ AL (Long Distance)**: Late deliveries are caused by **Carrier Delays (88.06% of late orders)**. Sellers are shipping on time, but carriers are failing.
   * **Route MA ➔ SP (Regional)**: Late deliveries are caused by **Slow Sellers (87.10% of late orders)** taking too long to hand over items to the carrier.

### Recommendations:
1. **Carrier Intervention**: Shift volume away from underperforming carriers on long-distance lanes (specifically routes from SP to Northeastern states like AL, MA, PI, and CE).
2. **Seller Enforcement**: Enforce a strict 48-hour carrier handover SLA for merchants in Maranhão (MA) and Paraná (PR). Send automated warnings to slow sellers.

## 🎯 Executive Summary for the CMO / VP of Marketing
**Core Question**: Are we acquiring and keeping the right customers?

### Key Findings:
1. **Low Retention**: Our repeat order share is extremely low (average **3.5% to 4%** of orders). Customer acquisition costs (CAC) are likely unsustainable because we must repurchase our customer base every month.
2. **Value of Loyalty**: Returning customers spend significantly more:
   * **`Champions` (Repeat buyers)**: AOV is **357 BRL**.
   * **`New / Recent` (First-time buyers)**: AOV is **161 BRL**.
3. **Customer Satisfaction Drivers**: Ratings are strongly tied to logistics. When OTD% falls, review scores drop.

### Recommendations:
1. **Re-engage "At Risk" Customers**: Launch target email campaigns to the **1,305 customers** identified in the `At Risk / Can't Lose` RFM segment. They have high historical AOVs (~301 BRL) but haven't purchased recently.
2. **Post-Purchase Campaigns**: Introduce "next-purchase discount" vouchers inside the shipping box for the `New / Recent` segment (48.33% of our buyers) to drive repeat orders within their first 30 days.
